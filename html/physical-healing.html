How Does Physical Healing Work?

  • The practitioner scans the energy field to detect areas of congestion or depletion.
  • Using specific hand movements and techniques, they cleanse the affected energy centers and remove stagnant or diseased energy.
  • Fresh, clean prana is then projected into the body to energize and revitalize the affected organs and tissues.

This process helps:

  • Reduce pain and inflammation

  • Alleviate symptoms of chronic illnesses
  • Support the body during post-surgery healing
  • Improve immune system function

Conditions that can benefit:

  • Digestive problems
  • Muscle and joint pain (arthritis, sprains)
  • Migraine and headaches
  • Fatigue and general body weakness
  • Skin conditions and more
