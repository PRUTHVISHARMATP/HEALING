About Me

With over 20 years of dedicated experience in the field of energy healing, I have had the privilege of guiding countless individuals on their journey toward physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. My practice is deeply rooted in the principles of Pranic Healing, a powerful no-touch energy healing modality that works with the body’s innate ability to heal itself.

My journey began with a deep desire to understand the subtle energies that influence our health and well-being. Over the years, I have trained extensively under experienced mentors and mastered advanced energy techniques that allow for deep healing without physical touch. I hold certifications in Advanced Pranic Healing, Pranic Psychotherapy, Pranic Crystal Healing, Associate Pranic Healing, Feng Sui and Happiness Coach continue to update my knowledge to provide the most effective care for my clients.

As both a healer and a trainer, my mission is to empower others—to help them rediscover balance, release energetic blockages, and live with clarity, vitality, and purpose.

Whether you're seeking relief from stress and pain, deeper emotional healing, or a path to personal growth, I’m here to support you every step of the way.
