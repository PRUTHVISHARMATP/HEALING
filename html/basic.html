Basic Energy Healing

Basic Energy Healing is the foundational course in the Energy Healing system, designed for beginners who want to learn energy healing. Developed by Grandmaster Choa Kok Sui, this course teaches simple yet powerful techniques to scan, cleanse, energize, and balance the body’s energy system.

Key Highlights

  • Learn to feel and work with energy (prana).
  • Understand the chakras (energy centers) and their role in health.
  • Discover techniques to accelerate the body’s natural healing.
  • Practice self-healing and healing others.
  • Learn energetic hygiene for protection and well-being.

No prior experience is required—just an open mind and a willingness to learn. This course is the first step toward becoming a certified Energy Healer and exploring deeper levels of energetic and spiritual healing.
